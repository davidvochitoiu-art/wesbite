Legacy & Industry Languages

COBOL

COBOL is still used in banking, finance, government systems, and older business applications.

Fortran

Fortran is used in scientific computing, mathematics, engineering, simulations, and high-performance research.

Ada

Ada is used in aerospace, defence, aviation, and safety-critical software where reliability is extremely important.

Hardware Design & Learning

VHDL / Verilog

VHDL and Verilog are used to design digital circuits, processors, FPGA systems, and hardware logic.

Scratch

Scratch is used for beginners to learn programming concepts through blocks, animations, games, and simple projects.

Modern Niche Languages

Crystal

Crystal is a modern language with Ruby-like syntax and compiled performance.

Nim

Nim is a flexible systems language used for performance, scripting, and modern software development.

Elixir

Elixir is used for scalable web systems, real-time applications, and fault-tolerant services.

Solidity

Solidity is used to build blockchain smart contracts and decentralised applications.
